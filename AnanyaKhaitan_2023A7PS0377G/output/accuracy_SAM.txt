Higher accuracy is observed due to the model including the footpaths or the edges of the footpath in the mask(especially at night time).
This can be reduced by taking better quality pictures with a good camera, a larger dataset, and making accurate annotations.
(A few images were low in quality, taken from a different phone, just to check how the model performs. This led to slight inaccuracy)

The measure of accuracy here is very crude and just considers the area of the mask and the annotations and finds the accuracy based on that, but it gives us a general idea of the accuracy.
